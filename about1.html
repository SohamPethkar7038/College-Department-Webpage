About Computer Science Department

The Department of Computer Science was established in the year 1993 with the objective of imparting quality education in the field of Computer Science. The Department has modern facilities for teaching, learning and research. The Department offers a wide array of research opportunities and programs of study at undergraduate and postgraduate level. The Department started 3-year Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS) programme in the year 1993. The Department started 2-year M.Sc. Computer Science programme in the year 1995 with the aim to develop core competence in Computer Science and prepare the students to carry out development work, as well as take up challenges in research. Now we are offering various programmes BCA, BSc Cyber, BBA(CA), MSc(CA), MSc(DS) With rapidly evolving technology and the continuous need for innovation, the Department has produced quality professionals holding important positions in the IT industry in India and abroad.
